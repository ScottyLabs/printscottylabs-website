Print@ScottyLabs

A simple solution to printing on the go at CMU

Just send a document to print@scottylabs.org to add to your CMU print queue

  • PRINT

    To print a document, send it as an attachment to print@scottylabs.org from your @andrew.cmu.edu email account (or another account you have linked; see below). Currently, PDFs and plain text documents are supported. Other formats might work too, but are not guaranteed.

  • LINK

    To configure your account with print@scottylabs.org, send an email with "CONFIGURE" in the subject line (in all caps). In the message body, put one of the following commands on each line:

    • "LINK new_address@example.com" - starts the process to link an email address that we don't know about to the one you're sending from. Note that you'll have to confirm that the new address is yours before you can use it.
    • "UNLINK some_address@example.com" - starts the process of unlinking an email address from your account. Note that you can't unlink your andrew account from its own print queue.

  • HELP

    To get help, send an email to print@scottylabs.org with "HELP" in the subject line (in all caps).

Remember that this service is currently in beta. You may encounter bugs, broken features, or brief service outages as we continue to improve print@scottylabs.org. Use print@scottylabs.org for mission-critical or sensitive print jobs at your own risk.

For assistance, or to give us suggestions or feedback, please contact print-feedback@scottylabs.org.
